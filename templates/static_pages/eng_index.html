About Us

Who we are and what we do.

NGO The main purpose of the NGO Rozumne Misto is comprehensive promotion of democratic processes.

In particular, it includes:
- e-democracy processes with engaging citizens, local authorities, business and volunteers;
- creating and distributing best practices with engaging experts, mentors and scientific authorities;
- e-services for citizens and business.

We are open for cooperation with state and local authorities, trade unions, voluntary, veterans, youth and other public and charitable organizations working in Ukraine and abroad.

Activities

Our organization covering the several directions.

E-government

The development of e-government in Ukraine for communities to awaken society. City residents can monitor the activities of government and take part in the life of the city. Municipalities hear the community and make informed decisions.

E-services

Creating a convenient service to provide local services in electronic form on the principle of a single window with a powerful backend and electronic documents.

E-workflow

Automating business processes of local governments including electronic services. Providing tools and designers to create e-business processes.

Open budget

Information about income and budget spending of the community. Visualization helps residents to understand the budget and analytics tools provides instrument of strategic planning for the municipality.

Condominiums

Service development condominiums, publicity process management decisions. Automation of condominium's accounting and distribution of best practices.

Training and consulting

Trainings for deputies, officials and activists on issues of cooperation between them and implementing e-democracy in their communities. Consulting on the use of e-democracy tools to the needs of residents.
